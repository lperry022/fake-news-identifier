play_circle_outlineHow to use this tool
  1. Paste a news headline or link into the search bar on the home page.
  2. Click Analyze — the tool will process your input in real time.
  3. View the credibility score, verdict, and flagged keywords.
  4. Click Dashboard (available after signing up) to see your past searches.
insightsUnderstanding the results
  • Verdict — quick indicator of overall trust level.
  • Credibility score — 0–100 scale; higher = more trustworthy.
  • Source reputation — the domain’s label and color badge:
Trusted
Untrusted
Unknown

palette Score color scale:

0–39: Potentially Misleading 40–69: Needs Caution 70–100: Likely Trustworthy

info Scores are advisory — always verify important claims via multiple sources.

warningSpotting misleading content
  • Over-the-top headlines (ALL CAPS, emojis, extreme claims).
  • No author/date or suspicious “About” page.
  • Single anonymous source or cropped screenshots without links.
  • Statistics without methodology or context.
  • Images out of context — try a reverse-image search.
accessibilityAccessibility & privacy
  • Keyboard & screen-reader friendly (ARIA roles, sensible focus order).
  • No personal data required to analyze a headline or link.
  • Data from signed-in users is anonymised and can be deleted anytime.
