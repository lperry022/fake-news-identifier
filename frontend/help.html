How our credibility score works

Scores are advisory. Always verify important claims via multiple sources.

  • Keywords — we flag sensational or misleading terms.
  • Source reputation — Trusted / Untrusted / Unknown domain labels.
  • Fact-checks — optional lookups to third-party services.
Spotting misleading content
  • Over-the-top headlines (ALL CAPS, many emojis, extreme claims).
  • No author/date or opaque “About” page.
  • Single anonymous source or cropped screenshots without links.
  • Statistics without methodology or context.
  • Images out of context — try a reverse-image search.
Accessibility & privacy
  • Keyboard & screen-reader friendly (ARIA roles, focus order).
  • No personal data required to analyze a headline or link.
